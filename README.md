# B.Tech Career Guide - UI/UX Design Case Study

## Overview

B.Tech Career Guide is a mobile application concept designed to assist engineering students in planning their academic and professional growth throughout their four-year degree program. The application provides structured roadmaps, learning resources, certification guidance, project recommendations, and placement preparation strategies.

The goal of this project is to create an intuitive and accessible user experience that helps students identify what to learn, when to learn it, and how to prepare for internships and placements.

---

## Problem Statement

Many engineering students face challenges such as:

* Lack of a clear learning roadmap
* Information overload from multiple online resources
* Uncertainty about required skills for placements
* Difficulty identifying relevant certifications and projects
* Limited awareness of internship opportunities

As a result, students often struggle to prioritize learning activities and career preparation.

---

## Project Objectives

* Provide a structured year-wise learning roadmap
* Help students discover relevant certifications
* Recommend projects based on skill level
* Support placement preparation through curated resources
* Improve decision-making through a simple and intuitive interface
* Create a centralized career guidance platform for engineering students

---

### Secondary Users

* Recent graduates

* Academic advisors

---

## User Research Summary

### Research Method

* Student interviews
* Online surveys
* Competitor analysis
* Observation of common student challenges

### Key Findings

* Students lack a structured career plan.
* Most students depend on random online resources.
* Students want clear guidance for skills, certifications, and projects.
* Placement preparation often starts too late.
* Students prefer visual roadmaps over lengthy explanations.

---

## Design Thinking Process

### Empathize

Conducted user research to understand challenges faced by engineering students.

### Define

Identified the lack of structured career guidance as the primary problem.

### Ideate

Generated solutions focused on roadmap-based learning, resource organization, and progress tracking.

### Prototype

Created low-fidelity wireframes to visualize navigation and content structure.

### Test

Validated the design approach through feedback from students and iterative improvements.
